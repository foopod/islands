FAQ

Why?

As time has gone on things have become less and less obtainable for me personally. It seems now that owning a home in my country might be impossible goal for me and many others. This project is both a way for me to do something I love (procedural generation) and hopefully instill a little joy for others to own their very own Island.

How much will they cost?

Nothing, just pay gas and minting fee. There is a 1% royalty fee on future sales so that I can maintain and keep creating these.

How will Islands be distributed?

To avoid abuse Islands will be given to people via a wait list. Whenever new Islands are minted everyone on the wait list will have an opportunity claim theirs. The aim will be to create an excess supply to the demand.

You will have 2 weeks to claim them before they go back into the pool to be claimed by someone else.

How long will new Islands be minted for?

I want to ensure that anyone that wants an Island can get one, if there is interest then I will try to keep this up for as long as I can.

Will newly minted Islands be the same as previous ones?

Every new generation of Islands will be released a month apart. Each will be slightly different, perhaps different biomes, objects, colours - they will all be Islands.

Can I get more than 1?

To make it easier for each person to get one, the waitlist will be reserved only for people that don't already have an Island.

Yes, you could just sign up with a different email address, but this really just puts more stress on me the creator to create excess.

If there is demand for it, then I can create some scheduled drops in larger batches.
